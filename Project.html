Hussainiwala Border

The Pride of Firozpur


A Famous tourist attraction and a place that boosts the patriotic feelings of everyone who visits.

  • Site of the National Martyrs Memorial
  • Bhagat Singh, Sukhdev and Rajguru were cremated here on 23 March 1931
  • Also the cremation place of Batukeshwar Dutt as well as that of Singh's mother, Vidyawati
  • An annual fair takes place at the memorial on 23 March, which is the anniversary of Singh's death
